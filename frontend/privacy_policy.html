Privacy Policy

Introduction

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when using EasyAbroad.

Data Collection

We collect personal information such as your name, email address, proof of studies or internships, and payment details.

Use of Data

  • Create and manage your account
  • Provide consultancy services
  • Process payments
  • Send reminders
  • Improve the platform through analytics

Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • To process payments (e.g., via Stripe)
  • As required by law or in response to legal requests

Data Storage

  • Profiles older than 5 years may be deleted.
  • Verification documents (e.g., transcripts) are deleted after successful verification.

Data Retention

  • User Profiles: Stored for up to 5 years after last activity.
  • Payment Information: Retained for 7 years.
  • Messages and Chat: Retained for 3 years.
  • Verification Documents: Deleted immediately after verification.
  • Email Addresses: Retained while the account is active and up to 12 months after deletion.
  • Analytical Data: Anonymized and may be retained indefinitely.

Your Rights

You can access, update, or delete your data at any time by logging into your account.
