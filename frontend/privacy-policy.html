Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when using EasyAbroad.

2. Data Collection

  1. Name
  2. Email address
  3. Proof of study/internship
  4. Payment details

3. Use of Data

  1. To create and manage user accounts
  2. To provide consultancy services
  3. To process payments
  4. To send reminders
  5. To improve the platform and services

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties, except in the following cases:

  • 1. To process payments (e.g., via Stripe)
  • 2. As required by law or legal obligations

5. Data Storage

  • Profiles older than 5 years may be deleted
  • Verification documents are deleted after successful verification

6. Data Retention

  1. User Profiles: Stored for up to 5 years after last activity
  2. Payment Information: Retained for 7 years
  3. Messages and Chat: Retained for 3 years
  4. Verification Documents: Deleted immediately after verification
  5. Email Addresses: Retained while account is active and up to 12 months after deletion
  6. Analytical Data: Anonymized and may be retained indefinitely

7. Your Rights

You can access, update, or delete your data at any time by logging into your account.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at info@easy-abroad.de

9. Cookies

We use cookies to improve user experience and analyze traffic. You can manage your cookie preferences in your browser settings.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be communicated via the platform or email. Continued use implies acceptance of the updated policy.
